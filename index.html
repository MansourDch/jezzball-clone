JezzBall Clone

Level: 1 Lives: 3 Filled: 0%

Touch Controls:

  • Drag to move paddle
  • Tap "Start Line" to draw
  • Double-tap to pause

Controls

  • Desktop: Spacebar to draw line, Arrow keys to move paddle
  • Mobile: Tap to draw, drag to move paddle
  • Button: Tap "Start Line" button

Objective: Keep the ball bouncing with the paddle and fill the screen while clearing enemies. Each life lost when the ball falls below the paddle.
